Ingredients

  • 1 cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices bread
  • Butter

Method

  1. Heat butter in pan
  2. Butter 1 slice of bread and place in pan
  3. Place lots and lots of grated cheese on bread
  4. Place 2nd bread slice on top.
  5. Cook till cheese is melted
